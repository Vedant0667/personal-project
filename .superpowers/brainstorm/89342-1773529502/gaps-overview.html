Missing — high-impact gaps

CRITICAL
Contact / Footer
No way to reach you. No email, no GitHub link, no LinkedIn, no footer at all. This is the #1 conversion goal of any portfolio.
CRITICAL
Hero CTAs
No action buttons above the fold. Recruiters should see "View My Work" and "Get In Touch" immediately. Right now there's no clear next step.
HIGH
Skills / Tech Stack
Languages, frameworks, tools you use. Recruiters scan this section first to check fit. Currently buried inside project cards only.
HIGH
Downloadable Resume
A "Download Resume" button is expected. Any recruiter or program application will look for this. Universally cited as must-have.
HIGH
Social / GitHub Links
GitHub is the most important external credibility signal for a developer. LinkedIn too. Neither is linked from the portfolio anywhere visible.
NICE
Availability / Status Badge
"Open to internships" or "Available Summer 2026" badge. Immediately signals intent to recruiters browsing.
NICE
Project Filtering
Filter projects by type (web, mobile, hardware, nonprofit). Lets visitors quickly find what's relevant to them.
Summary
The most urgent gaps are the 2 critical ones — no contact section and no CTAs means visitors have nowhere to go after being impressed. Skills section and resume download are also near-universal expectations. Let me know your thoughts in the terminal.
